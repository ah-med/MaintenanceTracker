Maintenance Tracker

Make your request and monitor its status

We believe that faster maintenance/repair activity cycle in an organisation will improve the overall effeciency of the employees in an organisation. Maintenance Tracker app allows employees to make a request about their repair and maintenance needs and a maintenance officer can update the status of the request.

An employee creates a request and a maintenance officer responds to the request by updating its status, request status can be: Pending, Approved, Rejected, Resolved
